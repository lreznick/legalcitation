The Headset

Usage Tips

In order to master the headset, the user must learn how to send consistent and distinct signals to the headset. Using the Emotiv Control Panel, thoughts can be trained to move a cube in a 3d environment. Practicing in this environment will allow you to hone your skills and send the most easily detectable signals. The following tips will set you in the right direction
